AbsPressureToTemperature

Temperature Intermediate FunctionBlock v1.2.3

FBD Diagram

Description



The function obtains the temperature of specified gas from its absolute pressure.

This is the formula used: a2 Temperature = ------------------------- - a3 - gl logf(Pressure) - a1

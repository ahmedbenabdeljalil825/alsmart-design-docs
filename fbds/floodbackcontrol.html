FloodbackControl

Compressor Control Advanced FunctionBlock v1.2.3

FBD Diagram

floodbackimage.png

Description



The brick controls the risk of liquid flood back in VZH compressors, evaluating the level of this risk defining it as “warning” or “alarm”.
The principle of working is based on discharge super heat temperature, as much this value goes below a dynamic reference as much is the risk of flood back.
The dynamic reference value depends on absolute ratio between discharge and suction pressures.
Discharge super heat temperature and pressure ratio must be filtered to avoid wrong results due to instantaneous variations.

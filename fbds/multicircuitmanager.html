MultiCircuitManager

Compressor Control Advanced FunctionBlock v1.2.3

FBD Diagram

multicircuitmanager.jpg

Description



This block distributes the requested power to different circuits.

It takes in account the power availability of each circuit (normally coming from Sequencer block), considering also prevention power reduction (maybe coming from evaluation of pressures or other events), and if the circuit is in alarm.

Then, the MultiCircuitBlock distru√¨ibutes the power to circuit from the knowledge of usiNumOfStages (number of compressors or partialization) per circuit and the usiMinNumOfStages (minimum number of compressor to turn on). For example, if usiNumOfStages is 2 with a total compressor of 4, the minimum power assigned to that circuit will be 50%, to allow the turning on of 2 compressors.
